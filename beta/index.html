Welcome to the Beta Description

Any link to a steam game would suffice so I pulled the first one I saw.

Triggers:

Wishlist Button - Towards the bottom left, below the trailer of the game

  • Hover On
  • Hover Off
  • Click

Rules:

Wishlist Button

  • Hover On:
    • Cursor changes to pointer
    • Color fades to a gradient blue
  • Hover Off
    • Cursor changes back to default
    • Color fades back to original
  • Click
    • Update the user's wishlist with the newly added game
    • Button splits into two pieces and fades in the new text
      • "✓ On Wishlist" on the left
      • Small dropdown chevron on the right
    • Color stays gradient blue
  • Click Again
    • Update the user's wishlist and remove the game
    • Two button pieces come together to form one button
    • Text fades back to original

Dropdown Menu Options

  • Hover On:
    • Color fades into a gradient blue
    • Flips and points upwards
  • Hover Off:
    • Color fades back to original
    • Flips and points downwards

Modes:

Wishlist Button

  • Hover On:
    • Text box fades in from above wishlist button with "Get notified by email when your wishlisted items get released or are on sale.""
  • Hover Off:
    • Text box fades out moving upwards
  • Click:
    • Text box above the wishlist button shrinks and fades to show different text with "Remove from your wishlist"
  • Click Again:
    • Text box above wishlist button expands and fades back to original text

Dropdown Menu

  • Hover On:
    • Two options fade in
      • Manage your wishlist
      • Remove from your wishlist
  • Hover Off:
    • Mange your wishlist fades out
    • Remove from your wishlist fades out
